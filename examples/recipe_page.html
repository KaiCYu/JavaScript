Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise (try to find one made of only egg yolks, not whole eggs)
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground caraway
  • ¼ teaspoon ground sumac
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • Just a sprinkle of ground turmeric
  • Pinch of xanthan gum
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

Halal Guys Sauce

Descriptions

NYC Halal Guys Food Cart Style Chicken/Lamb Over Rice.

Directions

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, water, and lemon juice together until smooth and no lumps appear.
  2. Add caraway, sumac, cardamom, and turmeric; stir until combined.
  3. Add xanthan gum and mix until it thickens slightly.
  4. Add black pepper to taste.
  5. Store in the refrigerator. Use liberally.
